Welcome

We are a group of non-partisan and non-profit university researchers.
This HIT is part of a larger study that we are leading on MTurk over 2022-2025.


Instructions

  • This HIT has a base reward of $0 and a bonus of maximum $0.
  • Your total payment will depend on decisions you will take during the HIT.
  • The expected completion time is 10-15 minutes.


The task is divided into six parts:

  • Part 1: A short survey about yourself.
  • Part 2: Reading about and answering questions on a given topic.
  • Part 3: A decision task in which you can earn a bonus.
  • Part 4: A counting task in which you can earn a bonus.
  • Part 5: Answering a few opinion questions.
  • Part 6: An item recognition task in which you can earn a bonus.


Please be aware that:

  • All questions are mandatory.
  • You need to complete the entire study to receive your reward.
  • Reponses that appear random or negligent will likely result in HIT rejection.
