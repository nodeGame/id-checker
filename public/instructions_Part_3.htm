Part 3


In this part, you can earn an extra bonus and contribute to fighting climate change.

How does it work?

  • On the next 4 pages, you will be informed about a sum of money.
  • The sum of money will be different on each page.
  • On each of the 4 pages, you decide how much you want to keep for yourself and how much you would like to give to an NGO that fights climate change.

Important!
  • Only one of the 4 decisions will be implemented.
  • The implementation of the decision does not depend on how you choose to split the sum.
  • It is in your best interest to answer truthfully each time.
  • Later on in this survey, we will let you know which of the four decisions was implemented.
  • For the selected decision, we will transfer the sum you decided to the NGO. The rest will be added to your reward for this HIT.
  • Once the study has concluded, contribution receipts will be displayed on the webpage of our research group (webpage link).

Where would your money go?
  • In case you decide to contribute a positive amount, we will transfer the amount you decide to the World Resources Institute (WRI).
  • WRI is a global research organization that works on a wide range of environmental and sustainability issues, including climate change.
  • WRI has established collaborations with organizations and governments worldwide.
  • You are welcome to read more about WRI's scope and initiatives here: https://www.wri.org/.
  • The authors of this study have no financial or other interests that could be perceived as a conflict of interest with the WRI.
